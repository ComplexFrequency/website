Despite working closely with AI and LLMs, everything I write in this tiny internet corner was typed by me. Through writing, I hope to touch the minds of my readers, to (re)add some humanity to the Internet, and, most importantly, I hope to have fun.

Outside of tech I'm into mathematics, classical literature, swimming, chess, and a bunch of other random things. Also, I'm slowly getting better at living.

## Why this blog

I have to admit, I'm not great at documenting my work. I'm also not very good at writing down my thoughts. Lastly, I'm great at having ambitious ideas for personal projects, but absolutely terrible when it comes to working on them. I think working on these skills can help me feel more fulfilled, which is the main reason behind the creation of this blog. Let there be light.
